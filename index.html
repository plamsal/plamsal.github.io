The person behind the code

From Nepal to the leading edge of enterprise AI.

My journey started in Nepal and has led me to the leading edge of enterprise AI — but my core philosophy has always stayed the same: the best technology is built with clear, human purpose.

I'm Pratik Lamsal. As a Georgia Tech grad and AI Systems Builder, I shape technical architecture in healthcare environments where precision, reliability, and trust matter.

I build systems that make complicated workflows feel simple. Whether the problem is patient intake, missed calls, operational bottlenecks, or human connection, my goal is to create AI experiences that help people move faster with more clarity.

The real challenge is not just building something impressive. The real challenge is building something useful, trustworthy, and easy enough for people to actually adopt.
