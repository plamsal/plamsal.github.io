The person behind the code

From Nepal to the leading edge of enterprise AI.

My journey started in Nepal and has led me to the leading edge of enterprise AI — but my core philosophy has always stayed the same: the best technology is built with clear, human purpose.

I'm Pratik Lamsal. As a Georgia Tech grad and AI Systems Builder, I shape the technical architecture at Moffitt Cancer Center. My work lives where cloud infrastructure, healthcare technology, and agentic AI collide — a space where precision isn't just a metric; it's a requirement, and the margin for error is zero.

We are living through a massive shift. Recent Deloitte research highlights that healthcare leaders are rapidly moving agentic AI out of the pilot phase and into core operating models to stabilize workforces and improve care delivery. But as these autonomous systems scale, the real challenge isn't just writing code that replaces a workflow — it's building trust.

I design responsible, multi-agent ecosystems that reason alongside human teams, scale smoothly, and make people fundamentally better at what they do.
