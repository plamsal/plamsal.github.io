Overview

This is a personal website for Pratik Lamsal (plamsal.github.io). It is a static, informational site with no user accounts, no forms collecting personal data, and no e-commerce. This policy explains what minimal data may be collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

This site does not actively collect personal information. However, the following may be collected automatically by the hosting infrastructure:

  • Server logs: GitHub Pages (the hosting provider) may log IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited as part of standard server operations.
  • Google Fonts: Fonts are loaded from Google's CDN. Google may collect limited usage data per their own privacy policy.
  • Substack links: Links to blog posts on Substack open on Substack's platform. Substack's own privacy policy applies there.
  • Hugging Face: The "Connect with Pratik" agent link opens on Hugging Face's platform. Their privacy policy applies there.

Cookies

This site uses a single cookie (pl_cookie_ok) solely to remember your acknowledgment of this privacy notice. No tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies are used.

Third-Party Services

External links on this site direct to third-party platforms (LinkedIn, GitHub, Substack, Hugging Face, Gmail). Each platform has its own privacy policy that governs data collected when you visit them. This site has no control over and takes no responsibility for their practices.

Data Retention

No personal data is stored by this site. Server logs held by GitHub Pages are governed by GitHub's data retention policies.

Your Rights

Since this site does not collect or store personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. If you have questions, you can reach out directly.
